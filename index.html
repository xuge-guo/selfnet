DOT: Lifelike Quadruped Locomotion

Designed, fabricated, and programmed a quadruped robot from scratch. Built a MuJoCo simulator and trained a PPO policy (>50M steps) to produce fast and natural gaits. Achieved 0.40 m/s stable walking on hardware.

Roles: mechanical design, controls, ML; Tools: MuJoCo, SB3, Python, Raspberry Pi
